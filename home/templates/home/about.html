What is Zoetrope?

Zoetrope is a tool to facilitate the collection, analysis, and processing of Google Street View images across time and geographies.


Why is it called Zoetrope?

A zoetrope is a device that uses a series of static images along the inside of a rotating cylinder to create a sense of motion or to tell a story.
We hope that Zoetrope can similarly utilize static images to tell the stories of how places change over time.
